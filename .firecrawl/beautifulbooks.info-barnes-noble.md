## Barnes & Noble

[Barnes and Noble](https://www.jdoqocy.com/click-100208466-12366831?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.barnesandnoble.com%2Fb%2Fbarnes-noble-collectible-editions%2F_%2FN-2cnb) (B&N) is a publisher known for producing beautifully designed and affordable book series that appeal to collectors and casual readers alike. This page links to comprehensive reviews of their various series, exploring the craftsmanship, unique features, and literary selections that define each collection.

[![bn old style Hestia Header Image](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20300%20169'%3E%3C/svg%3E)](https://beautifulbooks.info/illustratedbibliographies/barnes-noble-collectible-classics-the-complete-list/original-series/)

### [B&N Original Series](https://beautifulbooks.info/illustratedbibliographies/barnes-noble-collectible-classics-the-complete-list/original-series/)

The original leatherbound clasics. Published 1992-2005.

[![BN children illustrated classics hestia header](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20300%20169'%3E%3C/svg%3E)](https://beautifulbooks.info/illustratedbibliographies/barnes-noble-collectible-classics-the-complete-list/childrens-classics/)

### [Illustrated Children's Classics](https://beautifulbooks.info/illustratedbibliographies/barnes-noble-collectible-classics-the-complete-list/childrens-classics/)

Unabridged classics featuring illustrations from the "golden age". Published 2013-2018.

[![BN rainbow classics hestia header](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20300%20169'%3E%3C/svg%3E)](https://beautifulbooks.info/illustratedbibliographies/barnes-noble-collectible-classics-the-complete-list/rainbow-classics/)

### [Rainbow Classics](https://beautifulbooks.info/illustratedbibliographies/barnes-noble-collectible-classics-the-complete-list/rainbow-classics/)

Silhouette designs on a brightly hued children’s classics. Published 2011-2012.

[![BN signature editions hestia header](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20300%20169'%3E%3C/svg%3E)](https://beautifulbooks.info/illustratedbibliographies/barnes-noble-collectible-classics-the-complete-list/signature-editions/)

### [Signature Editions](https://beautifulbooks.info/illustratedbibliographies/barnes-noble-collectible-classics-the-complete-list/signature-editions/)

Beautifully lettered covers of classic volumes. Published 2011-2012.

[![BN Children Deluxe Hestia Header Image](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20300%20169'%3E%3C/svg%3E)](https://beautifulbooks.info/illustratedbibliographies/barnes-noble-collectible-classics-the-complete-list/barnes-noble-deluxe-childrens-treasuries/)

### [Deluxe Children's Treasuries](https://beautifulbooks.info/illustratedbibliographies/barnes-noble-collectible-classics-the-complete-list/barnes-noble-deluxe-childrens-treasuries/)

Oversized, leatherbound and profusely illustrated ‘deluxe’ treasury editions. First released 2012.

[![BN Pocket editions hestia header sm](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20300%20169'%3E%3C/svg%3E)](https://beautifulbooks.info/illustratedbibliographies/barnes-noble-collectible-classics-the-complete-list/pocket-classics/)

### [Pocket Classics](https://beautifulbooks.info/illustratedbibliographies/barnes-noble-collectible-classics-the-complete-list/pocket-classics/)

Slipcased children's classics with bright new illustrations. First released 2018 (now OOP).

[![BN collectible classics mix hestia header](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20300%20169'%3E%3C/svg%3E)](https://beautifulbooks.info/illustratedbibliographies/barnes-noble-collectible-classics-complete-list/)

### [Collectible Classics: The Complete List](https://beautifulbooks.info/illustratedbibliographies/barnes-noble-collectible-classics-complete-list/)

Every single book in the leatherboun and collectible clasics series. First released 1992, and still going strong.
