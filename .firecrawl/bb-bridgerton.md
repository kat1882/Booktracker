### A Collector's Guide to the _Bridgerton_ series

Julia Quinn’s Bridgerton novels are a cozy cornerstone of Regency romance. Centered on eight close‑knit siblings moving through London’s ballrooms (and scandals) with plenty of wit, the series can be read in order or dipped into by favorite couple. Lady Whistledown’s society columns add a playful, gossip‑sheet frame that ties the books together.

They are hugely popular, in no small part due to the TV series adaptation, which premiered in 2020 on Netflix. There a lots of different editions, I'm just focusing here on the most popular ‘collectible' sets.

##### Main book sequence in order

1\. _The Duke and I_(Simon & Daphne)

2\. _The Viscount Who Loved Me (Anthony & Kate)_

3\. _An Offer From a Gentleman (Benedict & Sophie)_

4\. _Romancing Mister Bridgerton (Colin & Penelope)_

5\. _To Sir Phillip, With Love (Phillip & Eloise)_

6\. _When He Was Wicked (Michael & Francesca)_

7\. _It’s in His Kiss (Gareth & Hyacinth)_

8\. _On the Way to the Wedding (Gregory & Lucy)._

##### Companion Books

Each of the books have a “second epilogue”, which are collected in

9\. [_The Bridgertons: Happily Ever After_.](https://amzn.to/3W44CHV)

There are also two Lady Whistledown anthologies.

10\. [_The Further Observations of Lady Whistledown_](https://amzn.to/3W1BlgY)

11\. [_Lady Whistledown Strikes Back_](https://amzn.to/4nkJR69)

And the TV tie-in prequel novel is co-authored by Julia Quinn and Shona Rhimes.

12\. [_Queen Charlotte_](https://amzn.to/4nLL4Ei)(first edition hardbacks have a reversible dust jacket!)

##### Prequel Quartet

Set a generation earlier (late 1700s), the Rokesbys are the Bridgertons’ close friends and neighbors. This series follows Bridgerton relatives Billie, Poppy, Georgiana and the Rokesby brothers. It's loosely related, as it gives the vibe of the Bridgerton family origins and family history.

1\. _[Because of Miss Bridgerton](https://amzn.to/4h7sl3r)(George & Billie)_

2\. _[The Girl with the Make‑Believe Husband](https://amzn.to/479TfDt)(Edward & Cecilia)_

3\. _[The Other Miss Bridgerton](https://amzn.to/47iN8Oi)(Andrew & Poppy)_

4. _[First Comes Scandal](https://amzn.to/3KMRtjZ)(Nicholas & Georgiana)_.

#### First Printings - US paperbacks

The original first editions of the series published by Avon featured covers typical of mass market paperback romance novels of the noughties – with delicately pretty, pastel covers.
